Samhain Gathering 2025

Thursday, October 31, 2025 · 7:00 PM – 10:30 PM ET · Riverside Community Center, Hall B

Join us for our annual Samhain celebration as we honor the thinning of the veil between worlds. This sacred gathering includes a remembrance ceremony for those we have lost, communal divination, seasonal food and drink, and a closing ritual circle. All are welcome — no prior experience necessary.

What to bring: A photo or memento of a loved one you'd like to honor, a dish or drink to share (optional), and an open heart.
